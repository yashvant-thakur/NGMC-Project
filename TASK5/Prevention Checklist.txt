Prevention Checklist


STAY SAFE ONLINE CHECKLIST:

• Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
• Always verify website URLs before entering details  
• Use strong and unique passwords  
• Enable two-factor authentication  

• Never share OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
• Always double-check UPI payment requests  

• Report cybercrime on cybercrime.gov.in  
• Call helpline 1930 in case of fraud
