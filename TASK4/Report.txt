Poor digital communication can lead to misunderstandings and missed opportunities. For example, sending an email without a subject line or proper greeting may appear unprofessional and be ignored by the recipient. In academic or professional settings, this can create a negative impression.

To avoid such issues, emails should always include a clear subject, polite tone, and proper structure. This task helped me understand the importance of professionalism in online communication and how small details can make a big difference.
