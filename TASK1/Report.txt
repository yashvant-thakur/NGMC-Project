For this task, I used Canva to design a digital literacy awareness infographic. The infographic highlights the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and useful digital tools for students. I included key points such as using strong passwords, avoiding phishing links, and maintaining a professional online presence through platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn.

The design was kept simple and visually appealing using icons and short text to ensure clarity. One interesting aspect of this task was learning how to present information visually instead of writing long paragraphs. Initially, arranging the layout properly was challenging, but Canva templates made it easier to organize content effectively.

This task helped me understand how visual communication can make complex ideas easier to understand.
